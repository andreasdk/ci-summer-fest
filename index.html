Rosalía

Rosalía always had a vision: to open an unexplored path based on the flamenco tradition but that completely redefines new pop music. At a time where linguistic barriers are breaking down and non-English spoken music is acquiring global predominance, this artist from Sant Esteve Sesrovires has not settled with reinventing a conceptual imagery and aesthetic, but has also gone for boundless transcendental experimentation hand in hand with El Guincho.
